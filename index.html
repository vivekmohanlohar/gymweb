Our state-of-the-art gym facilities


Cardio Equipment

Our gym is equipped with a variety of cardio machines including treadmills, elliptical machines, stationary bikes, and rowing machines to help you burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health.

Strength Training Equipment

Whether you're a beginner or an advanced lifter, we have a range of free weights, resistance machines, and powerlifting equipment to help you achieve your strength goals.

Group Fitness Classes

Join one of our exciting group classes such as Yoga, Zumba, HIIT, or Pilates. These classes are designed to keep you motivated while enhancing your fitness in a group setting.

Personal Training

Get personalized attention and expert guidance from our certified personal trainers. Whether you’re aiming to lose weight, gain muscle, or improve performance, our trainers are here to help.

Recovery Zone

Relax and rejuvenate in our dedicated recovery zone featuring stretching areas, foam rolling stations, and massage therapy (available upon request) to aid in muscle recovery.

Locker Rooms & Showers

Our gym features clean and secure locker rooms with showers, available for your convenience. Fresh towels and toiletries are also provided to enhance your gym experience.

Nutrition & Wellness Bar

Fuel your workout with our healthy smoothies and protein-packed snacks available at the gym's nutrition bar. Perfect for recovery after a tough session!

24/7 Access

Our gym offers 24/7 access, allowing you to work out at your convenience, no matter the time of day or night.
